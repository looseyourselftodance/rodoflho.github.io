The look of Bacchus


Representation of the famous work, "The Triumph of Bacchus," by Diego Vélasquez.
This painting juxtaposes mythology with everyday life. The canvas represents the god Bacchus as the god offering men the wine that frees them temporarily from their problems. In Baroque literature, Bacchus is considered as an allegory to the liberation of man from the slavery of daily life.

2020
1m x 1m
Oil on canvas
