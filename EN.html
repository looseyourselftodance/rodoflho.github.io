Le regard de Baccus
Representation of the famous work of Diego Vélasquez: The Triumph of Bacchus
This painting combines mythology with everyday life. The painting represents Bacchus as the god who offers men the wine that frees them temporarily of their problems. In baroque literature, Bacchus is considered an allegory of the liberation from the slavery of daily life.
