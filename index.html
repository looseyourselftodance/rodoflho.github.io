The Orange Bay studios

Rebecca Timoteo founded The Orange Bay Studios with a vision to provide a creative and collaborative environment for artists and entrepreneurs. Through her passion and hard work, she has created a thriving community of talented individuals who share a love for art and innovation.

Since its establishment in Florence in 2022, The Orange Bay Studios has been dedicated to promoting artistic expression and providing high-quality design services. From stunning oil paintings and intricate sculptures to innovative branding and graphic design, the studio offers a diverse range of creative solutions for individuals and businesses alike.

Omoshi Tropical Sushi

Developing the Omoshi Tropical Sushi brand was an immersive experience that allowed me to explore my creativity and culinary skills. From crafting the brand identity and designing packaging to learning how to make sushi and preparing the food myself, I oversaw every aspect of the brand's development. By leveraging digital platforms like UberEats, I was able to share my passion for sushi with the world and build a loyal customer base.

Creating the Omoshi Tropical Sushi brand was a challenging but rewarding endeavor that pushed me to explore new creative and culinary horizons. From developing the brand identity and packaging to learning the art of sushi-making and preparing the food myself, I was deeply involved in every aspect of the brand's development. By leveraging digital platforms like UberEats, I was able to share my love of sushi with a wider audience and establish a successful and unique brand that combined my passion for design and food.

I undertook the development of the Omoshi Tropical Sushi brand as a means of funding my lifelong dream of studying art in Europe. Through hard work, dedication, and a passion for creativity, I built a successful brand that not only allowed me to hone my culinary and design skills but also raised the funds needed to pursue my artistic aspirations. From sushi-making to designing and marketing, every aspect of the brand's development was fueled by my unwavering commitment to realizing my dream.

Legacy Design - Brazil

Enrolling in the French Alliance French School in Brazil on a scholarship and junior stage was a transformative experience that opened many doors for me. Through the program, I developed my language skills and immersed myself in the world of graphic design, eventually progressing from a junior role to an art director. With the support of my mentors and colleagues, I grew to become a skilled designer, creating stunning frescoes and print designs that were displayed in public spaces. This experience gave me a unique opportunity to learn and grow, both professionally and personally.

During my time at Legacy Design, I managed a team of 5 designers and spearheaded efforts to increase design production to 10 per day, while also leading advertising campaigns. By understanding the functional and non-functional requirements of our customers, I was able to translate their business needs into a technical backlog that informed the design and implementation of digital solutions. I established best practices, methodology, and processes to streamline activities across graphic design, marketing, and social media. As a tech lead in graphic design, I mentored less experienced colleagues, helping to upskill their abilities.
